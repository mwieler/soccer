Information

The Benjamin Wieler Soccer Tournament takes place on the Saturday after Thanksgiving at Madison High School in Madison, New Jersey. It funds a scholarship established in his name [by Nicole Cecala and others].

Each year, a scholarship is awarded to a college-bound Madison High School senior.

Sign Up

Anyone who wants to is welcome to sign up for the tournament. We can accommodate two hundred people (forty teams of five). To begin, please enter your name and email address below.

A $50 donation is required to register a team ($10/player). Your donation funds a scholarship that is awarded each year to a college-bound Madison High School senior.
